# Craft mode

## Before building

- Confirm `DESIGN.md` is approved.
- Inspect existing tokens, components, assets, and implementation conventions.
- Use real content and proof; placeholders are not presentation-ready.

## Build passes

1. semantic structure and content hierarchy;
2. layout, typography, color, and imagery system;
3. interactions and realistic states;
4. responsive composition and content priority;
5. accessibility, performance, and technical cleanup;
6. browser critique and correction.

Preserve the approved direction's major ingredients. Missing signature composition, imagery, navigation treatment, CTA treatment, density, or motif is a blocking fidelity defect unless explicitly accepted.

Cover default, hover, focus-visible, active, disabled, loading, error, success, empty, long-content, short-content, and overflow states where relevant.
