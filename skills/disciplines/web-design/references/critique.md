# Critique mode

Review the rendered interface, not the implementation intention.

Ask:

- Does hierarchy reveal the page job within seconds?
- Is the design memorable for reasons connected to the product rather than decoration?
- Does composition vary meaningfully while preserving system coherence?
- Are typography, color, imagery, shape, and motion one register?
- Are proof and actions placed where uncertainty occurs?
- Does the layout compose at narrow widths rather than merely shrink?
- Are states complete and interaction feedback understandable?
- Which approved design ingredients are missing or weakened?

Report observed evidence, consequence, and the smallest material correction. Do not invent defects merely to demonstrate iteration.
