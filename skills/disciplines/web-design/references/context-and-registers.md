# Context and design registers

## Artifact boundary

- `PRODUCT.md`: audience, situations, offer, voice, physical-use context, references, anti-references, and register.
- `SITE-CONTRACT.md`: page jobs, content hierarchy, conversions, proof, behavior, and acceptance.
- `DESIGN.md`: approved visual direction, system rules, motifs, states, and deviations.

## Brand register

Use when design carries identity: marketing sites, campaigns, editorial experiences, portfolios, and high-consideration landing pages. Prioritize memorability, emotional resonance, composition, imagery, and distinctive rhythm without sacrificing conversion or readability.

## Product register

Use when design serves repeated tasks: dashboards, portals, tools, account surfaces, directories, and workflow interfaces. Prioritize clarity, density control, state design, navigation, predictable interaction, and operational accessibility.

## Hybrid register

Use when a site contains both identity-led marketing and task-led application surfaces. Keep shared tokens and voice, but allow different density, motion, and composition rules by surface. Record the boundary in `DESIGN.md`.
