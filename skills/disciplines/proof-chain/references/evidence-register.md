# Evidence register

## Classifications

- `verified fact`: a source directly supports the proposed wording;
- `client assertion`: the client supplied the claim, but it has not been independently verified;
- `strategic inference`: an interpretation that must be presented as interpretation;
- `unavailable`: required evidence does not currently exist or has not been supplied.

## Workflow statuses

Statuses track publication readiness rather than evidence type:

- `approved`: the classification and source support the permissible wording;
- `open`: evidence or clarification is still requested; the classification normally remains `unavailable` until supplied;
- `blocked`: the proposed wording must not be published.

Review every number, superlative, comparison, guarantee, certification, customer count and outcome claim.
