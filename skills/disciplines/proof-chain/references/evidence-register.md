# Evidence register

Recommended statuses:

- `verified`: source directly supports the proposed wording;
- `client-asserted`: supplied by the client but not independently verified;
- `inference`: a strategic interpretation that must be presented as interpretation;
- `open`: evidence requested but not supplied;
- `blocked`: wording must not be published.

Review every number, superlative, comparison, guarantee, certification, customer count and outcome claim.
