# Proof Chain

A **proof chain** is:

```text
claim -> evidence -> source -> permissible wording
```

## Rules

- Classify evidence as verified fact, client assertion, strategic inference or unavailable.
- Record evidence in `docs/site/evidence.md` with a stable identifier.
- Preserve source context and date where material.
- Wording strength must not exceed evidence strength.
- Testimonials prove the quoted experience, not every broader business claim.
- Absence of proof is a content constraint, not permission to invent confidence.
- When a claim is strategically valuable but unsupported, propose either a proof request or evidence-safe wording.

Use [evidence-register.md](references/evidence-register.md) for status and wording patterns.

## Completion criterion

Every substantive public claim is either linked to admissible evidence, explicitly classified as a client assertion, softened to an evidence-safe statement or blocked from publication.
