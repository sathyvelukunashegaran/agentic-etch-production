# Website Copy

Copy implements the page contract; it does not invent the strategy.

## Before writing

Read the relevant page contract, `CONTEXT.md` and evidence register. Surface missing strategy or proof before drafting final claims.

## Writing discipline

- Lead with the user's situation, desired change or decision-relevant outcome.
- Keep one primary idea per content block.
- Prefer specific language from the domain over generic benefit adjectives.
- Match the call to action to the user's readiness and the page's conversion path.
- Place proof next to the claim or uncertainty it resolves.
- Distinguish final copy from alternatives and unresolved placeholders.
- Attach evidence identifiers to claims during drafting; remove internal IDs only from the final public surface.

Use [page-copy.md](references/page-copy.md) for the working format.

## Completion criterion

The copy fulfills every message and proof obligation in the page contract, contains no unsupported substantive claim and gives the intended user a coherent next action.
