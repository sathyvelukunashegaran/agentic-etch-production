# Grill Website

Resolve product truth before specifying pages.

## Load first

Explicitly load `website-strategy` and `proof-chain`. Read existing `CONTEXT.md`, `PRODUCT.md`, the evidence register, and available business material.

## Interview discipline

- Ask one decision question at a time and provide a recommended answer.
- Explore the environment for facts; reserve questions for decisions.
- Challenge broad audiences, generic differentiation, undefined conversions, borrowed visual language, and unsupported claims.
- Update `CONTEXT.md`, `PRODUCT.md`, and `docs/site/evidence.md` as decisions crystallize.
- Keep page architecture and visual solutions provisional; those belong to `/specify-website` and `/shape-website`.
- Branch to cited research or a cheap prototype when conversation cannot resolve a material decision.

## Required resolution

Resolve business outcome, users and triggering situations, offer, differentiation, conversion intent, voice, proof posture, design register, references, anti-references, constraints, and explicit out-of-scope boundaries.

## Approval gate

Present `PRODUCT.md` and the strongest unresolved risk. Do not mark it approved until the human explicitly accepts it.

## Completion criterion

A fresh agent can explain the business, audience, offer, vocabulary, voice, evidence boundaries, and intended design lane without reconstructing the original conversation.
