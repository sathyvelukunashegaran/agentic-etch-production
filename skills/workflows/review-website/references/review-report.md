# Review report

For every finding record:

- severity: blocker, high, medium, low;
- review axis;
- page, viewport, state, and journey;
- observed browser evidence;
- violated contract or rule;
- smallest credible correction;
- disposition: open, fixed, accepted, blocked, or deferred;
- owner and verification evidence after correction.

End with verified scope, unverified scope, accepted deviations, launch blockers, and a shipping recommendation.
