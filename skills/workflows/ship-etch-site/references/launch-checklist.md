# Launch checklist

## Release safety

- backup or snapshot confirmed;
- rollback method tested or explicitly verified;
- production access and responsible owner confirmed.

## Critical journeys

- navigation;
- primary conversion path;
- forms and confirmation behavior;
- dynamic content and empty states;
- authentication or commerce paths when present.

## Search and sharing

- indexability policy;
- canonical URLs;
- titles and descriptions;
- sitemap and robots behavior;
- redirects;
- social preview metadata.

## Measurement and compliance

- analytics events;
- consent and privacy behavior;
- error monitoring;
- form delivery ownership.

## Rendered quality

- required responsive widths;
- keyboard path;
- contrast and focus;
- overflow and layout shift;
- console and network errors;
- performance budgets recorded by the project.
