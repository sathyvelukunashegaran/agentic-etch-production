# Delivery ticket contract

Each ticket records:

- stable identifier and user-visible outcome;
- included and excluded scope;
- page-contract references;
- design-contract references;
- proof identifiers;
- dependencies and blocking edges;
- required stack capabilities and disciplines;
- acceptance criteria and required browser evidence;
- artifacts that may change;
- completion and handoff requirements.

A ticket should fit one fresh agent context and deliver a coherent vertical slice.
