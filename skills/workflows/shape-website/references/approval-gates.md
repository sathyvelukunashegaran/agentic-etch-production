# Visual approval gates

## Shape brief

Confirm target surface, page job, content hierarchy, constraints, existing system, and register.

## Direction questions

Resolve typography character, color commitment, density, imagery, motion, interaction character, physical-use context, references, and anti-references.

## Visual evidence

Directions must differ materially in composition, atmosphere, hierarchy, and signature moves. Cosmetic palette swaps are not separate directions.

## Approval record

Record the selected direction, rejected alternatives, approver, date, delegated decisions, and remaining risks in `DESIGN.md`.
